WIKI KNIGHTS

Wiki Knights is a student organization at UCF that believes information can and should be available, free of charge. This organization aims to create and promote free, open textbooks for the higher education community as well as contribute to existing open communities such as OpenStax, Wikipedia, Pressbooks, and others.
